Serene Springs Kalutara


Set against the backdrop of lush palm trees and overlooking the azure waters of the Indian Ocean,
it's just a short drive away from Colombo, making it easily accessible, yet a world away from the bustling city life.









Serene Springs Haputale


Located on the outskirts of Haputale town, surrounded by mountains with breathtaking views of valleys
and tea plantations and allowing easy exploration of nearby attractions.









Serene Springs Hikkaduwa


A tropical paradise situated along one of Sri Lanka's most famous beachfronts
with easy access to coral reefs and nightlife while immersing yourself in the lively spirit of the coastal town.
